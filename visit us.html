Upcoming Events

ON VIEW: OCTOBER 14, 2023 - FEBRUARY 18, 2024

Seven art houses host Artist Rounds and Summer Studios.

Artist Rounds are a biannual event, with openings in March and October,
for visiting artists to display works that address a topic, question,
or challenge facing the community.

Summer Studios is a summer residency that provides opportunities for
emerging artists, at local colleges or universities, to transform the
art houses into temporary studios and engage with
Houston's Historic Third Ward.
