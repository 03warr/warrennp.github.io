JOIN THE HOPE

The Hope is the official young
supporters' group (ages 21 to 39)
of Project Row Houses. Through
Black culture centered programs,
intimate artists talks and studio
visits, as well as creative and
engaging special events, The Hope
provides a space for exploring social
sculpture, self-taught and
outsider art.
